Welcome

Hello and welcome to SnipDex. SnipDex enables you to find what you are looking for quickly by leveraging the resources of other machines connected to the SnipDex network. SnipDex does not track your search behaviour and does not censor your search results.

Or ... if you prefer tech-jargon: SnipDex is a peer-to-peer application that enables large-scale distributed Internet search.

Haven't installed a SnipDex client on your machine? Please do install it and within several minutes you can search the web. By participating you help yourself and others find relevant results more quickly.
